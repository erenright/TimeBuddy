Hourly Reminders

Sometimes there is a particular task you wish to do or be reminded about every hour or so, such as getting up and walking around to stretch our your legs. TimeBuddy has a feature that gives you a custom message once per hour. The "hour" starts as soon as TimeBuddy is started.

To specify an hourly reminder:

  1. Open the Settings window the same as when creating a task
  2. Select the Options tab
  3. Type in the message you wish to receive next to the Hourly Reminder check box
  4. Check the box to enable the reminder (or uncheck to disable)
